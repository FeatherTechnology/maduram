Loan EMI options

Pay your EMIs conveniently with a repayment plan that works the best for you. Choose from our two options-

Fixed EMI -

With the fixed EMI plan, your monthly installment amount remains the same throughout the loan tenure. This way, you know how much money you must set aside every month for the loan repayment and plan your monthly budget efficiently.

Structured EMI-

Expect your salary to increase in the future? Then, you must consider our Structured EMI plan. It allows you to pay lower EMIs in the beginning and gradually increase the repayment amount as your income increases.
